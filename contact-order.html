About Us

As a young girl, I always had this fighting stubbornness to be the best I could be. I struggled at the beginning but I knew that with a little attitude, I could achieve whatever goals I had, and my goal growing up was to work in the kitchen. I thought I would be a Chef at a fancy restaurant somewhere in Manhatten, but life has its own mind. So, I took a little detour and opened up my first little bakery in Schaumburg.
