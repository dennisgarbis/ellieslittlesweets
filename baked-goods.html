Desserts

Little Ellie's number one passion is making cheesecakes. She started at a young age, following along YouTube videos, reading books, and getting hands on experience from her grandmother. Over the years she has perfected the cheesecake. Along with her cheesecake, her brownie is a close second. Nothing is better than the gooey fudge that Little Ellie's brownies are made of. Welcome inside to a dessert paradise.

Daily/Monthly Specials

Every day and every month we have something special ready for you. It could be a freshly baked tiramisu or a chocolate fudge cake, to Christmas cookies or pumpkin cupcakes to celebrate Halloween. Whatever the holiday, we offer something for everyone.
